# Product

## Register

product

## Users

Chromium users who keep several tabs open and prefer to move between them without leaving the keyboard. They need to recognize the right tab quickly while staying focused on the page they are using.

## Product Purpose

Samsara provides a compact tab switcher that previews open tabs and cycles through them with configurable shortcuts. Success means the intended tab is easy to recognize and select with minimal interruption.

## Brand Personality

Focused, fast, and understated. The interface should feel familiar to users of native application switchers and remain secondary to the browsing task.

## Anti-references

Avoid ornamental launcher interfaces, dense browser-management dashboards, and interactions that require the pointer for a keyboard-first task.

## Design Principles

- Preserve keyboard flow from invocation through selection.
- Put the most useful tabs where users expect them based on recent activity.
- Keep previews legible without obscuring more of the current page than necessary.
- Use familiar switcher behavior and clear selection feedback.
- Prefer immediate state changes over decorative effects.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Maintain complete keyboard operation, visible focus and selection states, semantic dialog and listbox structure, readable contrast at WCAG AA, and reduced-motion behavior for any future animation.
